What information we collect
Through bot usage:
  • Guild id.
  • Guild name.
  • Guild roles. This includes their id and name.
  • Guild members. This includes their id, username and discriminator.
  • Guild messages. Their id and channel id may be stored after the execution of certain commands.
  • Guild emojis. Their id may be stored after the execution of certain commands.
Through website traffic:
  • IP address.
How we use your information
Your information is used to ensure the proper functioning of our services.
We take privacy very seriously, and therefore we don't share any of your information with third parties.
How we protect your information
All information sent within our services is encrypted both in transit and at rest.
How long we keep your information
Any information is retained for as long as it's needed for the purposes for which we collected it.
Whenever Eliza loses access to certain information (e.g. when a member leaves a guild), such information gets deleted from our storage.
Changes to this Privacy Policy
We may alter the contents of this Privacy Policy at any time. The date the last changes were published is always indicated.
